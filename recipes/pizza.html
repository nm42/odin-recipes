Pizza

Pizza is another favorite dish of mine to make.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
    • 2 cups Flour
    • .5 cups Water
    • 1 packet of Yeast
    • 1 tsp Sugar
    • Olive oil
    • 1 large egg
  • 24 oz can Pizza sauce
  • 1 cup Shredded mozzarella cheese

Recipe

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough

STOP, before you proceed, pre-heat your oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.


  1. Knead the dough
  2. Roll the dough into a circle
