Beef Stir-Fry

A delicious recipe for beef stir-fry.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs sirlion steak strips
  • 1 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 1 large yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 1 cup white or brown cooked rice
  • Optional: Sesame seeds for garnish

Recipe

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Heat oil in wok or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Place steak strips in wok and cook for 4 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and vegetables with a dash of soy sauce.
  5. Cook together for 10 mintes and serve over rice.

ENJOY!
