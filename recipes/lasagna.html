Lasagna

Lasagna is one of my favorite dishes.

List of Ingredients

  • Wide pasta noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • 1 lbs ground beef
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp black pepper
  • 1 clove garlic

Recipe

  1. Make the meat sauce;
  2. Cook the noodles;
  3. Make the ricotta cheese mixture;
  4. Layer the lasgna according to the recipe instructions;
  5. Cover the foil and bake;
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
